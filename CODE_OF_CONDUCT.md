# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of this project, pledge to make participation
in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We are committed to fostering a respectful, professional, and constructive
environment for collaboration and discussion.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and respect toward others
- Being considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Providing constructive feedback in a professional manner
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on improving the project and the quality of collaboration

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal, political, or ideological attacks
- Publishing private information of others without explicit permission
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a technical or
  collaborative work environment

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

The project maintainer is responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards of acceptable behavior.

The maintainer has the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that
are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and may take appropriate action
in response to unacceptable behavior.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including but not
limited to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Code reviews and discussions
- Project documentation and communication channels

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public or
technical discussions related to this repository.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer via GitHub:

https://github.com/Ozymandias089

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The privacy and security of the reporter will be respected to the extent
possible.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

The maintainer will follow these guidelines when determining appropriate
responses to violations of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact**  
Minor unprofessional behavior or use of inappropriate language.

**Consequence**  
A private warning explaining why the behavior was inappropriate and how it
should be corrected.

---

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact**  
Repeated or more serious violations of community standards.

**Consequence**  
A formal warning and temporary restrictions on participation in project
discussions or contributions.

---

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact**  
Sustained or severe violations of community standards.

**Consequence**  
A temporary ban from participation in project-related spaces.

---

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact**  
A clear pattern of abusive, harassing, or disruptive behavior.

**Consequence**  
Permanent removal from participation in the project.
